Process

A simple system for handling property issues without losing track.

Whether it is a leak, unpaid rent, property tax bill, vendor invoice or tenant request, the goal is to identify, coordinate, document and report.

STEP 01

Identify

The issue, invoice, tenant request, tax bill or deadline is received and reviewed.

STEP 02

Coordinate

The right tenant, vendor, owner or professional is contacted and next steps are arranged.

STEP 03

Document

Photos, messages, receipts, invoices, approvals and notes are kept organized.

STEP 04

Report

The owner receives a clear update showing what happened, what was paid and what is still open.
