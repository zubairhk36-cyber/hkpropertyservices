When something goes wrong, the process matters.

If there is a leak, repair issue, tenant complaint, or vendor follow up, the goal is simple: respond quickly, coordinate the right person, document the issue, and keep the owner informed.

Issue is reported

Tenant or owner reports a problem such as a leak, appliance issue, lock problem, heating issue, cleaning concern, or general maintenance request.

Urgency is assessed

The issue is reviewed to understand whether it is an emergency, an urgent repair, or a regular maintenance item.

Vendor is contacted

The appropriate vendor is contacted, access is coordinated, and quote or repair details are collected where required.

Owner is updated

The owner receives an update on the issue, expected cost, approval needed, timing, and next steps.

Work is documented

Photos, invoices, messages, approvals, and repair notes are kept organized for proper backup.

Follow up is completed

The tenant and owner are updated after the work is complete, and the repair is added to the property record.
